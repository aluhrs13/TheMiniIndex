Welcome to The Mini Index!

This is a crowd-sourced index of 3d printable files for minis, terrain tiles, and scatter terrain across different websites for use with tabletop games like Dungeons and Dragons. We have 50,000+ models tagged with the important attributes of the mini in order to make it fast and easy to find exactly what you're looking for.
